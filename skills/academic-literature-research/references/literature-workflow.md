## Screening Rules

- Prefer papers with clear data definitions and test design.
- Flag papers that omit transaction costs, turnover, or universe construction.
- Separate predictive evidence from portfolio tradability.
- Do not treat high backtest returns as reliable without leakage and cost checks.
