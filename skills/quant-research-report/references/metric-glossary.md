# Metric Glossary

## IC

Information Coefficient. Cross-sectional correlation between factor values at formation time and future returns.

## RankIC

Spearman rank correlation between factor ranks and future return ranks. More robust to outliers than Pearson IC.

## ICIR

Mean IC divided by IC standard deviation. Measures stability of predictive power.

## Quantile Return

Return of portfolios formed by factor quantiles. A useful factor should often show monotonic or economically explainable grouping.

## Long-Short Portfolio

Portfolio long high-score group and short low-score group, or the reverse if factor direction is negative.

## Turnover

Percentage of holdings or weights changed at rebalance. High turnover can destroy paper alpha after costs.

## Max Drawdown

Largest peak-to-trough loss during the sample period.

## Sharpe Ratio

Annualized excess return divided by annualized volatility. Must match the data frequency and cost assumptions.
